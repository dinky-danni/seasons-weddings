Privacy Policy

This draft privacy policy explains how Seasons Weddings may collect and use personal information when someone visits the website or sends a wedding enquiry.

Last updated: 10 June 2026. This page should be reviewed before launch and updated with the final company/legal entity details, form processor and analytics setup.

Who We Are

Seasons Weddings represents wedding catering by Season Catering and wedding venue packages at Season Quayside, based at 72 Commercial St, Leith, Edinburgh EH6 6LX.

Information We Collect

When you send a wedding enquiry, we may collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, wedding date, venue details, guest numbers, menu interests, kitchen facilities, tasting interest and any notes you choose to share.

If analytics, maps, embedded tours or social media tools are added later, the website may also collect technical information such as device type, browser type, approximate location, pages visited and cookie preferences.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to wedding enquiries and check availability.
  • To prepare quotations, menu guidance and venue viewing information.
  • To arrange tastings, calls or follow-up planning conversations.
  • To improve the website and understand which pages are useful.
  • To meet legal, accounting or administrative obligations.

Sharing Information

Information may be shared within the Seasons team where needed to respond to an enquiry or plan a wedding service. If trusted suppliers, venues, form processors, email providers, analytics tools or website hosts are used, information may be processed by those providers only where needed for the service.

How Long We Keep Information

Enquiry details should only be kept for as long as needed to respond, manage a booking, meet legal requirements or maintain business records. Final retention periods should be confirmed before launch.

Your Rights

You may have rights to ask for access, correction, deletion or restriction of your personal data. You may also object to certain uses of your data or ask for a copy of information you have provided.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Seasons using the details on the wedding enquiry page or call 01316 037 990.
