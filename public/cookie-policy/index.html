Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Seasons Weddings website.

Last updated: 10 June 2026.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on a device when someone visits a website. They can help a site work properly, remember choices or understand how visitors use pages.

Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies help the website work, support security, remember cookie choices and allow forms or basic site features to function. Cloudflare Turnstile may be used as a necessary form security check to reduce spam.

Preference Cookies

The site stores a cookie preference choice so visitors do not have to answer the same optional-cookie question on every page.

Analytics Cookies

If analytics are added later, optional cookies may help Seasons understand which pages visitors use, how people move through the site and where improvements are needed.

Embedded Content Cookies

If maps, videos, 3D tours, social media feeds or other third-party tools are embedded, those services may set their own cookies. Google Maps and similar optional embeds are blocked until optional cookies are accepted.

Managing Cookies

Visitors can manage or block cookies through their browser settings. The website also provides a cookie settings panel where visitors can accept or reject optional cookies and change that choice later.

Current Cookie Use

The current static website does not intentionally set marketing or analytics cookies. This policy should be updated if analytics, advertising tools or embedded third-party services are added.

Contact

For questions about cookies or privacy, contact Seasons through the wedding enquiry page.
