How it works

A calm, delicious step in the planning journey.

Before the tasting

Share your date, venue, guest numbers, menu preferences and any dietary requirements.

At the tasting

Enjoy canapes and a three-course meal as shown on your quotation, with time afterwards to chat through any changes.

After the tasting

If the tasting sparks new ideas, the team can update the quote where menu changes are needed.

Practical notes

Know the tasting details before you book.

  • Tastings take place at Season Quayside.
  • Tastings are currently listed at £160 per couple, with free-tasting conditions depending on guest numbers, booking terms and current offers.
  • Free or discounted tasting terms can be confirmed when you enquire.
  • Drinks are charged separately.
