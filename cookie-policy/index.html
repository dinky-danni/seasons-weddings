Cookie Policy

This draft cookie policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Seasons Weddings website.

Last updated: 10 June 2026. This page should be reviewed once analytics, maps, embedded tours, videos or advertising tools are confirmed.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on a device when someone visits a website. They can help a site work properly, remember choices or understand how visitors use pages.

Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies help the website work, support security, remember cookie choices and allow forms or basic site features to function.

Analytics Cookies

If analytics are added later, cookies may help Seasons understand which pages visitors use, how people move through the site and where improvements are needed.

Embedded Content Cookies

If maps, videos, 3D tours, social media feeds or other third-party tools are embedded, those services may set their own cookies. Optional embeds should be reviewed before launch and gated behind consent where required.

Managing Cookies

Visitors can manage or block cookies through their browser settings. If a cookie banner is added, visitors should also be able to accept, reject or change non-essential cookie preferences on the website.

Current Wireframe Note

The current static build does not intentionally set marketing or analytics cookies. This policy is included so the site has the correct structure ready for final integrations.

Contact

For questions about cookies or privacy, contact Seasons through the wedding enquiry page.
