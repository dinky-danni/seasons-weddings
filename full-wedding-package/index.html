Venue overview

A private space for the moments that matter most.

Ceremony

Say your vows with up to 50 guests in the Glass Conservatory.

Reception & dining

Gather up to 100 guests for dining, with the Seasons food and drink team looking after the meal.

Evening celebration

The fully licensed bar, sound system and dance space keep the evening flowing. The dance space is not suitable for ceilidh.

Capacity guide

  • Ceremony guests: up to 50 in the Glass Conservatory.
  • Dining guests: up to 100.
  • Evening hire starts from 4:30pm on current packages.
  • Basic setup is provided, with trusted decor specialists and photographers recommended.
